1. **All sources cited above are from November 2025 or later** (where specific November 2025 data is referenced even if published earlier)

2. **CQC Standards pages** (December 22, 2025) provide the most current regulatory framework references

3. **East Kent Hospitals critical incident** (January 12, 2026) is the most recent acute event backing Canterbury/East Kent page claims

4. **Fair Pay Agreement references** (November 24, 2025 publication of September 30, 2025 announcement) are essential for all pages referencing workforce changes
